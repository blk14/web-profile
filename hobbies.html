Basketball

It was an important part of my lifetime with great achievements, but also with downsides. Food&Cooking

   Cooking is almost one of my daily activities and I love doing it when I have an idea of cooking something new. This hobby comes from an unmeasurable pleasure of eating good and mouthwatering food. When it comes to travel I'll always think about the gastronomy from that country or region and I would definetly go for local foods and products. The persons who inspire me in terms of cooking techniques are Gordon Ramsey, Jamie Oliver, Gennaro Contaldo and Akis Petretzikis.
